Yeti3D Old

Port of the "Yeti3D Old" 3D engine techno demo by Derek J. Evans from Nintendo Game Boy Advance (GBA) to SDL2 and Emscripten.

Technical Information: 35 FPS, 220x176 (viewport) => 320x240 (screen).

Controls:

  • Arrows and W, A, S, D – Movement.
