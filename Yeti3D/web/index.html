Yeti3D

Port of the heavy optimized "Yeti3D" 3D engine techno demo by Derek J. Evans from Nintendo Game Boy Advance (GBA) to SDL2 and Emscripten.

Technical Information: 35 FPS, 320x240 (viewport) => 320x240 (screen).

Controls:

  • Arrows and W, A, S, D – Movement.
  • C, V and Page Up, Page Down – Look up, Look down.
  • X and Space – Jump.
  • Z and Left Ctrl and Enter – Fire.
